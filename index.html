Autoresearch Standards (Applied to All Skills)
📐 Scoring
• Weighted formula: passing weights / total weights × 100%
• Critical = 3x, High = 2x, Normal = 1x
• 5 dimensions: Completeness, Accuracy, Actionability, Timeliness, Format
• Minimum bar: 75% before going live
🔄 Improvement Loop
• One change per round (never two)
• Keep if score ↑, revert if ↓ or flat
• Stop at: 90%+ for 3 rounds, or 8 rounds no improvement
• Log everything in changelog
🧠 Meta-Autoresearch
• Triggers: plateau (3 rounds <5%), feedback gap, every 4 weeks
• 5 analyses: stale Qs, impossible Qs, gaps, weight recal, proposals
• Checklist changes are versioned and reversible
• Re-baseline after any checklist change
